Tetris

A simple Tetris (C/SDL2)

Tetris

Controls

  • Left / Right / Bottom : move tetromino
  • Top : rotate tetromino
  • Space : drop tetromino
  • p : Pause
  • m : music

Get Tetris

Standalone Tetris is available here for Windows, Linux (soon) and OSX (soon).

Or you can compile it yourself, see below.

Dependancies

SDL2 library

  • SDL2
  • SDL2 ttf
  • SDL2 image
  • SDL2 mixer
